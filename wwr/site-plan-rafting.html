Purpose

Rafting allows you to delve deeper into nature and discover a completely new world in the water.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
Rafting is suitable for all people, it is advisable to bring a medical examination indicating that you do not have heart problems because it is an extreme sport, for our safety and that of the client. It's not just for experts. By this we mean that you can still practice this sport on an individual level without having knowledge of it, as you can go with your children, recommended from the age of 5, you can also go with your family.

What do they want?
Rafting is an adventure sport and as such it consists of descending whitewater rivers. What they want is to live the adventure with friends or family so that it is a unique experience. Normally between 6 and 8 people can go accompanied by the guide who is located at the back of the boat, from where he directs it.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
What they often expect to find are the prices, if there are any promotions, what services part of the rafting we provide. There we leave our link to our website so you can know in detail all the services we provide and the price.
